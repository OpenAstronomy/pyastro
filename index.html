    Python in Astronomy

    Python in Astronomy is a conference series focusing on the use, development and community surrounding Python packages for all types of Astronomy. To date the conference has been an annual event, with an edition in 2016 and 2015.

    2017

    The 2017 edition will be back at the Lorentz Centre in Leiden on the 8-12 May 2017. For more information click here.

    2016

    The 2016 edition of Python in Astronomy was hosted at the University of Washington eScience Institute in Seattle, WA, USA.

    For details on the 2016 edition click here.

    2015

    The 2015 Python in Astronomy conference was on the 20-24 April 2015 at the Lorentz Centre in Leiden. For more information and links to the proceedings click here.
