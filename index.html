    Python in Astronomy

    Python in Astronomy is a conference series focusing on the use, development, and community surrounding Python packages for all types of Astronomy.

    2019

    This year's Python in Astronomy conference will be at the Space Telescope Science Institute in Baltimore on July 29th - August 2nd.

    2018

    The fourth meeting was held at the Center for Computational Astrophysics in New York on the 30th April - 4th May 2018. More details can be found here.

    2017

    In 2017, the conference was back at the Lorentz Centre in Leiden on the 8-12 May 2017. For more information click here.

    2016

    The 2016 Python in Astronomy was hosted at the University of Washington eScience Institute in Seattle, WA, USA.

    For details on the 2016 conference click here.

    2015

    The 2015 Python in Astronomy conference was on the 20-24 April 2015 at the Lorentz Centre in Leiden. For more information and links to the proceedings click here.
